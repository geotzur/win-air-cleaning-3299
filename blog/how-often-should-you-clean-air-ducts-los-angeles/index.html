How Often Should You Clean Your Air Ducts in Los Angeles?

Most HVAC experts recommend cleaning your air ducts every 3 to 5 years, but Los Angeles homeowners often need to clean more frequently. Between seasonal wildfire smoke, Santa Ana wind dust, and urban particulate pollution, LA duct systems accumulate contaminants faster than homes in other parts of the country. If you have pets, allergies, or live near a major road, the buildup happens even faster.

Watch for these signs that your ducts need attention: visible dust blowing from registers when the system starts, a musty or stale smell when the HVAC runs, increased allergy symptoms indoors, and higher than normal energy bills. If you have recently renovated your home, construction dust in your ducts is almost guaranteed. A camera inspection can tell you exactly what is inside your system.

Win Air Cleaning recommends an initial inspection for any Los Angeles home that has not had professional duct service in the last three years.
