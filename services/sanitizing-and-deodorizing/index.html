About This Service

Even after a thorough duct cleaning, bacteria and odor-causing compounds can linger on duct surfaces. Our sanitizing and deodorizing service applies an EPA-registered antimicrobial fog throughout your entire duct system, killing bacteria on contact and neutralizing odors from pets, cooking, smoke, and mustiness.

We use a ULV (ultra-low volume) fogger that disperses a fine mist evenly through every branch of your ductwork. The treatment coats interior surfaces and continues to inhibit microbial growth for months after application. It is safe for use in homes with children and pets, and it leaves no chemical residue or strong fragrance.

This service is especially popular with Los Angeles homeowners who have recently moved into a new home, completed renovations, or dealt with water damage. It is also a great add-on after a standard duct cleaning for families with allergies or sensitivities. Win Air Cleaning only uses products that are EPA-registered and proven effective.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the sanitizing treatment safe for my family?

Yes. We use only EPA-registered products that are approved for use in occupied spaces. The treatment leaves no harmful residue and is safe for children, pets, and people with sensitivities.

How long does the sanitizing effect last?

The antimicrobial coating continues to inhibit bacterial growth for 3 to 6 months depending on conditions. We recommend pairing it with regular duct cleaning for ongoing protection.

Will this remove cigarette smoke smell from my ducts?

It significantly reduces smoke odor in most cases. For heavy or long-term smoke contamination, we may recommend a full duct cleaning followed by multiple sanitizing treatments for the best results.
