Dryer Vent Cleaning
in Pasadena

Clogged dryer vents cause nearly 3,000 house fires in the United States every year, and lint buildup is the leading cause. A blocked vent also forces your dryer to work harder, increasing energy costs and shortening the appliance's lifespan. Dryer vent cleaning is a simple service that protects your family and your wallet.

We disconnect your dryer, access the vent line, and use a high-powered rotary brush system combined with compressed air to remove every bit of lint from the duct. We clean from the dryer connection all the way to the exterior exhaust hood. After cleaning, we reconnect your dryer, test airflow at the outlet, and verify proper exhaust function.

Many Los Angeles homes have long or winding dryer vent runs that trap more lint than standard setups. Our team handles vents routed through walls, ceilings, and rooftops. We recommend cleaning your dryer vent at least once a year, or more often if you do heavy laundry loads.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if my dryer vent is clogged?

Common signs include clothes taking longer than one cycle to dry, the dryer feeling unusually hot to the touch, a burning smell during operation, and visible lint around the exterior vent hood. If you notice any of these, call us right away.

How often should dryer vents be cleaned?

At least once per year for most households. If you have a large family, do frequent laundry, or your vent run is longer than 15 feet, twice a year is safer. We can assess your setup and recommend the right schedule.

Can you clean dryer vents that go through the roof?

Yes. Our team has the equipment and experience to clean roof-vented dryer lines safely. We access the vent from both the interior and exterior to ensure complete lint removal.
