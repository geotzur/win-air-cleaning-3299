Services Available in West Hollywood

Residential Air Duct Cleaning

Complete cleaning of your home's ductwork using NADCA-approved source-removal methods. We clear dust, allergens, pet dander, and debris from every supply and return line so your family breathes cleaner air.

Commercial Air Duct Cleaning

Professional air duct cleaning for offices, retail spaces, restaurants, and commercial buildings across Los Angeles. We work around your schedule to minimize disruption to your business.

HVAC System Cleaning

Deep cleaning of your entire HVAC system including the air handler, blower motor, evaporator coil, and condensate drain. A clean system runs more efficiently and lasts longer.

Dryer Vent Cleaning

Lint and debris removal from your dryer vent line to prevent fire hazards, reduce drying times, and extend the life of your dryer. One of the most important safety services for any home.

Indoor Air Quality Testing

Professional air quality assessment to identify pollutants, allergens, mold spores, and VOCs in your home. Know exactly what you are breathing before deciding on a treatment plan.

Mold Remediation in Ducts

Identification and removal of mold growth inside your air duct system. We eliminate the contamination and apply antimicrobial treatments to prevent it from returning.

UV Light Air Purification

Installation of UV-C germicidal lights inside your HVAC system to continuously neutralize bacteria, viruses, and mold spores as air circulates through your home.

Electrostatic Filter Installation

Upgrade from disposable filters to permanent, washable electrostatic filters that capture more particles and save you money over time. Better filtration with less waste.

Sanitizing & Deodorizing

EPA-registered antimicrobial treatment applied throughout your duct system to eliminate bacteria, neutralize odors, and leave your home smelling fresh and clean.

HVAC Maintenance Plans

Scheduled annual or semi-annual HVAC and duct maintenance to keep your system clean, efficient, and long-lasting. Priority scheduling and discounted rates for plan members.
